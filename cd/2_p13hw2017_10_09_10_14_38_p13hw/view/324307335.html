Major battle looms in cash transfer industry


The People Daily

31-Aug-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Gitahi Ngunyi

Page : 18

AVE : 232229

Summary
The country's lucrative and pioneering mobile money transfer business is taking a new shape as commercial banks and card payment service providers angle to win a piece of the pie. CA statistics show that Safaricom had a 91.6 per cent dominance of mobile commerce business. Mastercard unveiled its own app which operates in much the same way as Visa.
