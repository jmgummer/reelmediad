62 ATMs removed as phone takes over


Business Daily

29-Aug-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Brian Ngugi

Page : 19

AVE : 85500

Summary
The number of automated teller machines (ATMs) decreased by 62 or 2.3 per cent to 2,656 by last December from 2,718 a year earlier. Central Bank of Kenya (CBK) said the drop was driven by adoption of cost effective channels such as the use of mobile and agency banking.
