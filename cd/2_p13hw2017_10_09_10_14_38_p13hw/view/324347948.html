Summary
Sometime in June 2016, Catherine Mutua checked her bank account at Equity and realized that Sh100,000 was missing. Catherine wrote to The Nairobian Defender and gave shocking details of her untold suffering in the hands of the bank.Three weeks ago, The Nairobian Defender wrote a passionate appeal to the Equity Bank boss James Mwangi after waiting for more than a year to get some cases resolved. Since the letter was published, The Defender desk has been inundated with complaints from Equity Bank customers.
