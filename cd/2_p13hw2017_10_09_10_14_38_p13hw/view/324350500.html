Lenders face fresh pressure over credit


The East African

16-Sep-2017

Type : Weekly Newspaper

Journalist : Maryanne Gicobi

Summary
Kenyan banks are expected to shy away from lending due to the cap on borrowing rate and a new set of global accounting rules. The CBK is in the process of reviewing the interest rate cap.
