Credit Bank's net profit up 8% to Sh92 million


The Star

25-Aug-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Cynthia Ilako

Page : 15

AVE : 59297

Summary
Bottom tier lender Credit Bank beat a loss-making wave in the banking industry to post an 8.51 percent increase in net profit during the first half of the year. Barclays has so far posted the largest decline of 13.45 per cent followed by NIC Bank at 12.2 per cent.
