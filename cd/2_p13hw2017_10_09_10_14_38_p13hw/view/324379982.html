Summary
The banking amendment Act 2016 came to force close to a year ago earnest on the need to spur private sector growth, to make loans cheaper and offer a much needed reprieve for majority who couldn't access credit due to its perceived overpricing, it has however met outright opposition from the banking sector. The CBK recently said it is pushing for the repeal of the banking amendment Act 2016.
