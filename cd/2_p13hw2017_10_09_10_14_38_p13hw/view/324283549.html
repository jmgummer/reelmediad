Equity Bank to stop offering salary loans


Business Daily

23-Aug-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : David Herbling

Page : 1

AVE : 325697

Summary
Salaried workers and owners of small businesses are headed for tough times after Equity Bank announced it is cutting down unsecured and micro loans to comply with a new set of global accounting rules on loan loss covers. Equity, which is Kenya’s biggest bank by customer numbers with a client base of 11.7 million, announced a 7.4 drop in net profit. Growth of credit to the private sector fell further to 2.1 per cent over the 12 months to May 2017, according to CBK data, blamed on the rate caps and pre-election jitters. Equity has traditionally focused on micro-enterprises and low-income earners to grow volumes and overtake giants such as Barclays and StanChart, who previously dominated Kenya’s banking scene.
