Warriors, Blades battle at Strathmore


The Star

23-Sep-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Dann Owerre

Page : 34

AVE : 57319

Summary
Former national champions Ulinzi Warriors take on Blades in a tough Premier League clash at the Strathmore University Courts today. The students, who are third on the table with 32 points , are coming off a morale boosting 79-66 victory over Trailblazers .
