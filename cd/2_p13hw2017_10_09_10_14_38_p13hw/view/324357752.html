Court reduces pain for lenders hard-hit by high fraud instances


Daily Nation

19-Sep-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Abiud Ochieng

Page : 33

AVE : 218558

Summary
Banks have lately been victims of fraudulent deals by their staff and cybercriminals who employ smart tactics to fleece the institutions of millions of shillings. The Central Bank of Kenya has introduced cybersecurity guidelines aimed at helping lenders deal with cybercrimes and prepare for emerging threats. In March this year, several hackers were arrested among them KRA staff in the ICT department.
