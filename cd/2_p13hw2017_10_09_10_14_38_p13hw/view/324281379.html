What is driving Safaricom stocks at Nairobi bourse?


The Standard - Financial Standard

22-Aug-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Jevans Nyabiage

Page : S6

AVE : 1029372

Summary
Safaricom shares touched an all-time high of Sh25 at NSE. The telco faces competition from Airtel and Telkom Kenya. Safaricom partnered with Carepay to provide health services under M-Tiba. M-Tiba are managed by UAP Insurance. Barclays, Equity Bank, EABL, BAT, KCB, Bamburi are among 10 most valuable companies at the bourse.
