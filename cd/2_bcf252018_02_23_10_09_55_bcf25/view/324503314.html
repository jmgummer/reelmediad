SHORT TAKES: WICKED FARMERS


Daily Nation

08-Nov-2017

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : JUSTIN N. NKARANGA

Page : 16

AVE : 14633

Summary
Chicken food should not be laced with HIV and Aids antiretroviral drugs as some farmers do. To make quick and huge profits, some poultry farmers have a tendency to lace chicken feed with the drugs. They serve it as a fattener for the chicken to gain weight quickly in preparation for slaughter. If you are already doing this, please stop. It is devilish to go to whatever length to lace human food with unwanted drugs, just for the love of money.
