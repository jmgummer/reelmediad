Kenya Power recovers cash from fallen Imperial Bank


Daily Nation

08-Jan-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Mugambi Mutegi

Page : 29

AVE : 76093

Summary
Kenya Power has recovered Sh34.1 million from Imperial Bank as part of approved payments by the Central Bank of Kenya following the lender's collapse in October 2015. The receipt of the funds, which the NSE-listed electricity distributor discloses in its latest annual report for the financial year to last June, leaves the company with Sh288.3 million stuck in the failed lender.
