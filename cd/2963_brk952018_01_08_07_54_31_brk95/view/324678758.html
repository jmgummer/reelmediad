Bourse investors book billions as bear run ends


Business Daily

08-Jan-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Charles Mwaniki

Page : 12

AVE : 707403

Summary
Investors holding bank and telecommunication Safaricom shares at the stock market had the biggest cheer at the end of last year as these segments returned a double-digit growth to earn them additional billions in paper wealth. The energy segment gained Sh21.7 billion to Sh120.6 billion, helped by KenGen whose share price rose by 47.4 per cent in the period to Sh8.55.
