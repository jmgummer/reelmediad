Tanzania exports more goods to Kenya in 10 months to October


Daily Nation

08-Jan-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Constant Muinda

Page : 29

AVE : 250410

Summary
Tanzania sold more goods to Kenya in the 10 months to October, in spite of cutting back on consumption of Kenyan-made products. Data by the Central Bank of Kenya shows that imports from Tanzania increased by 25.44 per cent to Sh13.264 billion in the January-October 2017 period compared to a year earlier. KAM has also released its data on products which have been subjected to re-labelling in Tanzania.
