Summary
The popular Safaricom Jazz Festival hosts its fifth edition at the Kasarani Stadium Training Grounds this Sunday with a brilliant performance from be the highly acclaimed American jazz trio BWB, made up of Grammy Award winners Norman Brown (guitar) and Kirk Whalum (saxophone) and trumpeter Rick Braun.
