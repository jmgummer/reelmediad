Parents storm school, demand head's transfe


The Star

11-Jan-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : CHARI SUCHE

Page : 30

AVE : 35922

Summary
Angry parents stormed a primary school in Kinango and demanded the immediate transfer of the head teacher and his deputy, citing poor performance. Ndauni Primary School parents said the school had posted poor results over the last four years, with girls often dropping out because of pregnancies. Kwale county education director Brigit Wambua confirmed the dispute. She said they had drafted transfer letters for the two teachers and were waiting for the TSC director to sign them.
