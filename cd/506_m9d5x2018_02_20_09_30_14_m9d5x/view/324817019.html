In Kenya, between 2000 and 2010, 37.4 million diverted cigarette exports were intercepted by KRA, with a value of Sh 174 million and estimated tax loss of Sh111 million. Illicit trade is often a cross-border act, especially where there are tax and price differentials between two countries. e protocol to eliminate illicit trade in tobacco, known as the Illicit Trade Protocol, was adopted by the Conference of the Parties to the WHO Framework Convention on Tobacco Control at its fi fth session in Seoul, Korea.
