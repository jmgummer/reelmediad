Irony of low tax revenues amid increased alcohol consumption and players


The Standard - Financial Standard

20-Feb-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Paul Wafula

Page : S7

AVE : 630523

Summary
Domestic excise duty collections recorded the worst performance in the first half of the year among all tax classes, declining by nine per cent despite a growing number of alcohol importers and manufacturers.Latest tax figures released by the KRA says the drop is a departure from the previous three years that posted an annual average growth of 16 per cent.
