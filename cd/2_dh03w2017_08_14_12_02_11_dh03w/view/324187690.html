Summary
Janet Mbugua from Unilever discussing about their Heroes for Change program which is a social mobilization volunteer programme that aims to teach and empower college students to contribute to society. The initiative that aims to reach 10 million Kenyans by 2020, has brought together different partners among them Amref and United Nations Population Fund.
