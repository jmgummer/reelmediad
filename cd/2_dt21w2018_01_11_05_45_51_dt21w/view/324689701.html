City Hall accuses KeNHA of illegally cutting trees along Mombasa Road


Business Daily

11-Jan-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Lillian Mutavi

Page : 6

AVE : 67721

Summary
City Hall and the Kenya Forest Service have raised concern over the cutting of more than 5,000 trees along Mombasa Road by the highways agency. Nairobi county government says the Kenya National High ways Authority (KeNHA) fell the trees during the festive season last month from Nyayo Stadium to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport ( JKIA) without authority from City Hall.
