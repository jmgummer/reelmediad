E-taxi drivers protest at Miritini SGR terminal


The People Daily

10-Jan-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Harrison Kivisu

Page : 17

AVE : 94529

Summary
Online taxi-hailing firms drivers on Monday paralysed transport services at the Miritini SGR terminal as they protested over alleged harassment by authorities manning the station. The drivers, who include those from Uber, Taxify and Little Cab accused the police and the Kenya Railways Corporation management of allegedly colluding with cartels to kick them out of the station where five picking slots have been allocated for them.
