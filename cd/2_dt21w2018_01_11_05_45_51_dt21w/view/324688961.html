Summary
Directors of State corporations are set to get a monthly pay increase of up to 87.5 per cent in a new plan signed off by the Treasury, putting into doubt the government's commitment to tame its runaway wage bill, among the affected bodies include the Central Bank of Kenya, Kenya National Examination Council,KenGen among others.
