Fault from Ol Karia caused nationwide blackout


NTV

10-Jan-2018

Time : 00:03:32

Length : 21:39

AVE : 1171880

Summary
75% of Kenya was thrown into darkness just as the sun was going down, Tuesday evening. Energy Cabinet Secretary Charles Keter confirmed that the 6 hour outage also affected supply to Uganda. And just like the very same date last year, the outage was caused by a fault on the line from Ol Karia.
