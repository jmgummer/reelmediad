Sunday deadliest day on Kenyan roads in 2017


Business Daily

10-Jan-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Keziah Kinuthia

Page : 12

AVE : 723693

Summary
Sunday was the deadliest day for travelers on Kenyan roads in 2017, breaking a trend from previous years when most fatalities were recorded on Friday and Saturday. Fresh data by the National Transport and Safety Authority (NTSA) shows that 595 fatalities occurred on Sunday in 2017 the highest among days of the week. In 2016, the highest number of fatalities was recorded on Saturday at 545 victims.
