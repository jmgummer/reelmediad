Safaricom rehires top staffer in Dar job hitch


Daily Nation

28-Sep-2018

Type : Daily Newspaper

Journalist : Patrick Alushula

Page : 33

AVE : 114606

Summary
Safaricom has rehired former director of consumer business Sylvia Mulinge after her appointment as the managing director of Vodacom Tanzania ran into a hitch over a work permit snag. Ms Mulinge joined Safaricom in 2006 from Unilever where she served as Sunlight brand manager. At Safaricom, she rose to the position of director, consumer business in March 2015.
